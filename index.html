About Us

Natural Ponni Agro Products is a family‑run spice manufacturer from Thiruvaiyaru, Thanjavur, bringing you hygienically processed turmeric and other staple spices. We source directly from farmers and follow minimal, natural processing so you get pure colour, aroma and taste.

  • Own processing & packing
  • No artificial colours or additives
  • Wholesale & retail supply
